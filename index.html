Japan Real Estate Prices Analysis

Julien Desautels, Jake Tian, Guan He

Introduction

We intend to analyze the principal drivers of real estate prices in Tokyo / Japan (scope TBC). Our dataset provides a selection of real estate features and sale prices over time. The project identifies which features predominantly impact sale prices and evaluate their comparative significance in property valuation.

Ultimately, we aim to forecast property values based on a defined set of attributes, leveraging our analysis to inform potential investors, real estate agents, policymakers and home owners of the major drivers of Tokyo / Japanese real estate prices in a viewer-friendly manner.

Data Sources

The primary data source for this project is the Japan Real Estate Prices dataset available on Kaggle.
